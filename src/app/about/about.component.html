About Us

‘Student services and facilities management system’ is there to make student life more comfortable and convenient towards leading a delightful experience so that student will not be worrying about worthless things. We will empower them to live more productive in academics as well as explore all dimensions of student life full fledgedly. We will be providing information of services and facilities available for students like nearby hostels/ accommodations, canteens/food centres, Hospitals, Gyms, ATMs, Stationaries, Xerox centres etc on one platform at one click.

We personally have faced this problem of not having clarity regarding these leading to waste of time as well as misguiding or not receiving deserved return on investments. Achieving this objective is tedious, difficult and outdated by manually enquiring to endless number of people as the information is scattered, can be redundant and collecting relevant, updated information may be very time consuming.

But no worries, we are there to connect you with the best!
